What is this site?

ISP Monitor finds your home IP address and monitors your internet provider's uptime. We check uptime every 5 minutes and store results from the past 24 hours. When we see an anomaly such as high latency or packet loss, we store the result for 7 days.

Where do I sign up?

There is no sign up process. We use your home IP address to provide you with the proper information. Your public IP address is available to anyone on the internet.

How do I start?

Go to your ISP page. If this is your first time accessing this page, it will take 5 minutes for the site to gather your first results.

Where are my results?

Results are added every 5 minutes. You will need to allow ping/ICMP to your home firewall so that our servers can ping you.

What are anomalies?

Anomalies are results outside what we would expect to see when running our tests. We mark results as anomalous if latency is above 300 ms or packet loss is above 0%.

How much does it cost?

The site is free for everyone. If you want, you can donate to help keep the servers up.
